These poems were written collectively in Fall 2024 and by Paul, Rosalee, Anne, Tomorrow, Nick, William, EJ, Jorge, Maria, Josh, Ilan, Rachel, Peter, and more.
Our weekly themes were sound, momentum, repetition, renewal, truth, solidarity, love, fear, and forgiveness.
We read work by Audre Lorde, Aracelis Girmay, Ross Gay, Terrance Hayes, Billy-Ray Belcourt, Natalie Diaz, Zaina Alsous, aja monet, Mohammed El-Kurd, bell hooks, Pablo Neruda, Gwendolyn Brooks, and Mary Oliver.
We performed our work in December 2024 @ Wychwood Open Door. Special thanks to Nick, Janelle, and Neye.
